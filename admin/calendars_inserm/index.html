Calendars

Go to the site :

//webmail.inserm.fr/cal/index.html#?refresh=1568626616955

On the calendar, select the desired time slot and click on edit details:

At the top right, indicate the name of the room and then give a title to the reservation (Group meeting for example).

In the top right corner, remember to indicate the room’s reference so that the reservation appears in the room’s calendar:

Enter “res-318@” or the name of the room to find it:

Otherwise make the request to Monique Frei (monique.frei@…)

EDIT:

In order to simplify the online booking of the rooms of the regional delegation, their identification will be modified as follows as of Tuesday, September 29, 2020 :

DROM-PS0: small room on the ground floor

DROM-GS0: large room on the ground floor

DROM-PS1: small room on the 1st floor

DROM-GS1 : large room on the 1st floor

** DROM: Regional Delegation Occitanie Méditerranée.*
