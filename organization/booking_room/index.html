Booking a CBS room

Log in with you credentials (should be the same as your webmail):

https://resa.cbs.cnrs.fr/RESA/day.php?area=8&id_site=1

Select Sites/Domaines/Ressources and click of the “+” in the calendar to add your booking:

Booking a room @INSERM

Go to the following site and login with your Inserm credentials :

//webmail.inserm.fr/cal/index.html#?refresh=1568626616955

Go to the Agenda tab and select the desired time slot and click on edit details:

At the top right, indicate the name of the room and then give a title to the reservation (Group meeting for example).

In the top right corner, remember to indicate the room’s reference so that the reservation appears in the room’s calendar:

Enter “res-318@” or the name of the room to find it:

  • DROM-PS0: small room on the ground floor
  • DROM-GS0: large room on the ground floor
  • DROM-PS1: small room on the 1st floor
  • DROM-GS1 : large room on the 1st floor

** DROM: Regional Delegation Occitanie Méditerranée.*

As a reminder, in order to reserve a meeting room, you just have to add it as a participant:

You can also consult the planning of the rooms by adding the calendar of one or all the rooms to your views:
