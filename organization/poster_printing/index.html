Poster printing

Quote

Get quote (demande de devis) to one of these copy shops:

  • Digitalis : jfulep@digitalis-impression.fr

​ 04 67 10 00 56

  • Copyshop : production@copyshop.fr

​ 04 67 47 35 29

  • http://www.espacecopie.fr/ (can be done online)

These copy shops deliver to the CBS (according to Florence, no idea how long that takes)

Purchase order

Sent quote to Florence, she prepares a purchase order (bon de commande)

  • ask Christophe next time to prepare the bon de commande

  • it’s also necessary to know which funding (Episcope, coût direct)

Sending pdf

For Espace Copie, send them the final poster per pdf

  • sending after lunch, the poster was finished around 17h30 the same day

  • standard poster is 30€ (fabric would be 40€, I haven’t checked the quality)

  • when getting the poster, take the bon de commande with you

  • you should get a bon de livraison in return
